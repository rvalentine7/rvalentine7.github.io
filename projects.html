Edge of Infinity

Edge of Infinity is a 2D puzzle game where the player has control of a various number of characters and must try to get them safely to the exit at the end of each level. There are a number of hazards and obstacles along the way that the player will need to think around in order to advance. This game was created in Unity with a group of people from VGDev (Videogame Developers). I worked as a programmer to help code the obstacles and characters found in the game.

Gallipoli Model

The Gallipoli peninsula was the location of a battle in World War 1 which involved British, ANZAC (Australian and New Zealand Army Corps), and Turkish forces. Gallipoli is a prime example where British and ANZAC command structure broke down. Using this background, the multidisciplinary team of people from Engineering, International Affairs, and Computational Media wanted to see what outcomes would result from giving troops more autonomy in a battle. I primarily worked on coding the model while my teammates worked on figuring out what parts of the battle needed to be represented. We worked together to figure out how the different elements of the battle would be represented in the model.

The video shown here depicts the best case scenario we found for giving troops more autonomy. The red dots on the screen are the ANZAC forces, the blue dots are the British forces, and the green dots are the Turkish forces. The black triangles are objectives the ANZAC and British are trying to reach and the Turkish are trying to force the ANZAC and British off of the peninsula.

One Night Only


Rules for One Night Only

One Night Only is a board game where each player is a thief who has obtained the security codes to a museum. These codes will reset soon which means the thieves have only one night to make their move and collect as many valuables as they can. The thief who collects the most is declared to be the greatest. Each thief is able to place traps around the museum to hamper their opponents' progress in an attempt to make sure he/she is declared to be the greatest thief. This game was created with two other people for my Game Design class.

Game AI

This game is a top-down shooter which requires the player to destroy the enemy base before his/her own base is destroyed. The enemies will adapt after each round in an attempt to counter the player's strategy. This game was created alongside one other person for a project in my Game AI class. The game was coded in Python using Pygame. I coded the procedural obstacle placement, enemy movements, waypoints, and navigation mesh.

Full Moon

Full Moon is a 2D platformer I created for my Media Device Architecture class. The player controls a werewolf and must escape the dungeon. Along the way, the player will encounter obstacles he/she must fight or jump over to advance in the levels. The game is coded in C and uses a Gameboy emulator to run. I fully coded Full Moon from the ground up.
